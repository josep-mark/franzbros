About Franz Bros LLC

Matt Franz has over ten years of experience in landscaping and lawn care management. In May of 2015, he graduated from Rutgers University, Camden with a degree in Business. Matt has since used his business skills to grow Franz Bros LLC

Franz Bros LLC began in 2008 when Matt, Chris, and Joe Franz started the business by caring for neighborhood lawns.
